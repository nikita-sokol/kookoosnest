Our location

We are nestled in a tiny private cove of white sand on the very tip of Antulang peninsula, facing the sunset over the sulu sea . Set into a backdrop of wooded slopes and shear cliffs, in an area of outstanding beauty, we consider Kookoo's nest to be an "Eco-lodge" as much as a resort.

Getting here

Getting to Kookoo's Nest is part of the adventure! We are located on the southern tip of Negros Island, a little over an hour from the city of Dumaguete. We will be glad to help you pick the best transport options to reach us from anywhere in the world.

See the Links page for some useful transport websites.

Once you arrive in Dumaguete, you can make your way here by taking a bus or jeepney to Zamboangita, then change to a habal-habal motorbike to Kookoo's Nest at Antulang Village. Or do it the easy way - call us to arrange for a taxi to pick you up from the airport, the ferry pier, or your hotel, to bring you straight to Kookoo's Nest.
