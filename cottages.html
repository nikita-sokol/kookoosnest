Our bamboo cottages

Six rustic bamboo beach cottages situated in our own secluded cove on the very tip of Antulang peninsula. Kookoo's Nest offers simplicity, relaxation and recreation on a sheltered white sand beach, with fantastic coral reefs, stunning sunsets and great food.

Frangipani

Centrally located and as the name suggests, built above the Library, there are two floors and great views out to sea.

Details:
  • Double bed on the lower floor;
  • Double bed in the top floor loft;
  • Private bathroom;
  • Balcony with hammock;
  • Great views out to sea;
  • Free wi-fi in the restaurant.
Price: ₱1150

Cottage prices are for two people.

There is an additional charge of 200 pesos per extra person.
