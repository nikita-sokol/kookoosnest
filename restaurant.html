The restaurant & bar

Our kitchen is the heart of Kookoo's nest, and the casual open-air beach Restaurant is a great place to watch the fisher folk by day and the sunset by evening while enjoying great food.

Cooler shady mornings make for a relaxed leisurely breakfast, with fresh brewed coffee, home-baked bread, fresh tropical fruits, or a big fry up with fresh eggs and some home cured smoked bacon.
Our menu is extensive and varied, with International cuisine using the freshest produce and herbs from our garden, cooked to order by our local staff.

In the evening the restaurant has a communal atmosphere, offering a supper buffet with guests enjoying the homely feel of eating together. This also gives us an opportunity to use seasonal fruits, vegetables and seafood and dishes not on the menu. The supper buffet and communal table are purely optional, there are also individual tables for a more intimate meal, you can feel free to join us or not, as the mood takes you.
