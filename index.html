Welcome to Kookoo’s Nest

We are nestled in a tiny private cove of white sand on the very tip of Antulang peninsula, facing the sunset over the sulu sea . Set into a backdrop of wooded slopes and shear cliffs, in an area of outstanding beauty, we consider Kookoo's nest to be an "Eco-lodge" as much as a resort.

Kookoo's Nest offers recreation and relaxation on a beautiful white sand beach with stunning sunsets and great food. We have six bamboo beach Cottages in a range of sizes to suit families, couples and single travelers. The cottages are surrounded by a pocket of natural woodland with many species of tropical trees and plants including towering coconut palms. As a result you may see many kinds of birds from your cottage balcony or the restaurant, including Kingfishers, Sunbirds, Fantails and many others, so bring your binoculars.

For recreation, we offer coastal walks, swimming in the clear waters of our private beach and kayaking along the coast, exploring Tambobo Bay and the mangroves. A beautiful coral reef starts just a few meters from the beach, so there are excellent opportunities for snorkeling, and we offer a range of Scuba Diving experiences for beginners to experienced divers, including PADI courses.

About us

We are Jamie and Nikki Ingram, we have owned and operated Kookoo's nest since 2001. Our staff are all from the local village of Antulang, just a short walk away. We aim to be a benefit to this isolated community and endeavor to keep the proceeds of Kookoo's nest in the local area. We have built our cottages from Bamboo and Nipa palm, both are sustainable and renewable materials, we try to minimize our impact on the environment.
